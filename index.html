12-month horizon

What Changes

⚡ Speed

What feels fast today will feel slow in 12 months. AI compresses cycle times from idea to production.

↗ Scope

What required multiple people or weeks of work may be achievable by one person in days.

↓ Cost

Building and maintaining gets cheaper. This changes how we think about investment and trade-offs.

✦ Quality

Speed does not come at the expense of quality. It raises the bar. Fix more, including the small paper cuts.

Disciplines will blur. Designers ship real UI. Engineers shape product ideas. Fewer silos, more cross-contribution.

Eyes open

What We're Watching For

Cognitive overload

Guiding agents is intense. We need to be conscious of the mental load on builders.

Accountability drift

People may shift responsibility onto AI. This is not acceptable. We own our systems — AI does not.

Complexity creep

We could build more than we can maintain. Discipline in what we choose to build matters more, not less.

Principles must transfer

Our engineering principles (avoid big bangs, instrumentation, etc.) must evolve into the agentic workflow.
